About District

Bulandshahr, located in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India, is a historic city with roots that stretch back centuries. It is known for its rich cultural heritage and its significance during the Mughal era. Situated about 80 kilometers from the national capital, New Delhi, Bulandshahr is an important city in western Uttar Pradesh.
